Etch-a-Sketch

Welcome to Etch-a-Sketch, where you can draw with just your mouse! Please select a grid size below to generate a canvas. The more squares you select, the more detail you can add to your drawing.

The screenshot button will copy your drawing to your clipboard and the reset button will erase your canvas so you can start over.

Planned features

  • The ability to select colors
  • A gradient rainbow mode
  • A randomized rainbow mode
  • A user-submitted gallery
